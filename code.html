Shahi Paneer

Shahi paneer is a preparation of paneer, native to the Indian subcontinent, consisting of a thick gravy of cream, tomatoes and Indian spices.

Price: ₹ 175

Chole Bhature

Chole bhature is a food dish popular in the North India. It is a combination of chana masala (spicy white chickpeas) and bhatura/puri, a deep-fried bread made from maida.

Price: ₹ 200

Dal Makhani

Dal makhani is a dish originating in New Delhi,India. A relatively modern variation of traditional lentil dishes, it is made with urad dal (black beans) and other pulses, and includes butter(makhan) and cream.

Price: ₹ 125

Dosa

A dosa is a thin pancake in South Indian cuisine made from a fermented batter of ground black lentils and rice.

Price: ₹ 150

Upma

Upma is a dish originating from the South India cooked as a thick porridge from dry-roasted semolina or coarse rice flour.

Price: ₹ 140
